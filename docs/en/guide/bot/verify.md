# Authorization
This Bot's maimai features require authorization. Authorization scope is per group.

:::danger Unauthorized Notice
If you add the bot to a group without prior approval, the bot will not provide maimai-related services.
:::

## Free Authorization

You can obtain free authorization through the following methods:
- Arcade groups: Official arcade player groups that have an official designation
![](/images/official_arcade_example.png)
- Local groups: Groups of a certain scale whose primary members are local players
![](/images/local_arcade_example.png)

If your group meets the above criteria, please [send us an email](mailto:awmc@awmc.cc) with the following information:

> Subject: Group number + "Arcade Authorization Request"
> 
> In the body, include the arcade address + a screenshot of the group info page.

## Paid Authorization
If your group is a personal hobby group or rhythm game community group, please visit https://store.awmc.cc/ to purchase group authorization.
